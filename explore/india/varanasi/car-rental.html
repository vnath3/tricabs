    Explore Varanasi with Axis Cabs

    Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Banaras, is one of the oldest living cities in the world and a major pilgrimage destination for Hindus. Situated on the banks of the sacred river Ganges, Varanasi is known for its spiritual significance, vibrant culture, and rich history.

    Varanasi is home to numerous temples, ghats (riverfront steps leading to the river), and ashrams, making it a hub of religious and cultural activities. The city is known for its Ganga Aarti, a mesmerizing ritual that takes place every evening on the ghats, where priests perform prayers with fire as an offering to the river Ganges.

    Apart from its spiritual significance, Varanasi is also famous for its rich cultural heritage. The city is known for its classical music, dance, and arts. The bustling streets of Varanasi are filled with shops selling traditional handicrafts, silk sarees, and other items, making it a paradise for shoppers.

    For foreign tourists, Varanasi offers a unique and immersive experience of Indian culture and spirituality. From exploring the ancient temples and ghats to witnessing the Ganga Aarti and indulging in local cuisine, Varanasi offers a glimpse into the heart and soul of India.
